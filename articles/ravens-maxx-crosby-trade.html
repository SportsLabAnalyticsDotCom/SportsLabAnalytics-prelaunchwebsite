Ravens Nix Trade For Raiders OLB Maxx Crosby

The Maxx Crosby deal is off, according to the Raiders.

Trade Deal Falls Through

The Baltimore Ravens and Las Vegas Raiders had been discussing a potential trade for star outside linebacker Maxx Crosby, but those talks have now ended. According to sources close to the Raiders organization, the deal is officially off.

Crosby's Value to the Raiders

Maxx Crosby has been one of the Raiders' most consistent defensive players since being selected in the fourth round of the 2019 NFL Draft. The edge rusher has developed into a Pro Bowl caliber player and has been a cornerstone of the Raiders' defensive unit.

Ravens' Defensive Needs

The Ravens were reportedly interested in adding Crosby to bolster their pass rush, which has been an area of focus for the team as they look to remain competitive in the AFC North. However, the two sides were unable to come to terms on compensation.

What This Means Moving Forward

With trade discussions concluded, Crosby will remain with the Raiders for the foreseeable future. The Ravens will need to look elsewhere to address their defensive needs, potentially through free agency or the NFL Draft.
